【无授权英译】 It was so cold

发表于2020-10-05|更新于2020-11-12|Star WarsFanFic翻译
|阅读量:

无授权英译，无beta，请谨慎阅读。

Ship: Darth Vader & Luke Skywalker , Darth Vader / Luke Skywalker

Author/卓燃Hibana

Translator/Arita

Sidious always requested him to kneel for saluting. The acute pain resulted from his prosthesis limbs grinding his barely remained tissues acted as a painful reminder and a mock of his past days as a slave. While Sidious was quite satisfied about his own disgusting doings, Vader, however, was clearly aware that his rage towards these unfair treatments would only lead to his further fall to the abyss. Therefore, he chose to deliberately ignore the disgrace of repeated kneeling before others, and settled himself into a lamentable calmness , too enough to be numb. Now, He was indeed too numb to realize the darkness that used to swallow everything no longer existed.

“You aren’t going to do that again, Father.”

Luke, whose limbs were slim but powerful, sounded tender and firm. He now radiated like a star.

“Please take your seat, Father.” He pointed to the only seat in the throne room.

Vader lowered his helmet slightly, and said,”That’s your position, young one.”

“No—None of us will need it,” Luke’s tone sounded as clear as water, “We will restore the Republic.”

Vader seated himself on the throne. Despite being sheltered by his life-maintaining suit, Vader could still feel the deepest coldness originated from the surroundings. The darkness, which had been reigning here for decades, never faded away. In fact, he himself was driven by the seduction of this darkness in his early years, which eventually turned himself into a killing machine. Gradually, however, he began to feel that the coldness dissipating, for his star was closing in.

Luke could look into Vader’s eyes now, his height finally reached his father’s. He gazed at the dark helmet and into the obscure lens, anyhow he did have a sense of being embraced by some kind of tenderness appeared to be clear blue, just like calm water in the lakes. He tried to touch that tenderness, and then he gained a silent acceptance. So he seated himself on Vader’s thighs.

“Do you hurt? “ Luke asked cautiously.

Vader didn’t answer, just opened his arms to hug his child, softly.

It was a long missing scene between them, indeed.

Luke buried himself into his father’s chest. He could clearly distinguish the damaged but still powerful heartbeats from the chaotic breaths.

“The new Republic shall sentence me to my deserved fate.”

Neither sorrow nor happiness could be found in Vader’s tones, because he had already made his heart to accept his fateful endings and his trials, as well as his separation from his children, again.

“Well……leave alone that. “

Both Luke’s body and his presence in the Force shivered a bit. He felt cold. He was by no means ignorant, he was just unwilling to accept it.

Out of solace, Vader hugged his child more tightly. Luke let out a slight sigh and closed his eyes.

Just take a rest, Vader thought.

Assuming it was a universe that let go of the control of time and destiny.

FIN.
