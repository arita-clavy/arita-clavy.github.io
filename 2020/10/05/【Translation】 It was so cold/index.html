【Translation】 It was so cold

本文最后更新于：几秒前

This is the English translation of “【Vader &/ Luke】好冷”, written by 卓燃Hibana。

无授权英译，无beta，请谨慎阅读。

[Translation] It was so cold

Ship: Darth Vader & Luke Skywalker , Darth Vader / Luke Skywalker

Author/卓燃Hibana

Translator/Arita

Sidious always requested him to kneel for saluting. The acute pain resulted from his prosthesis limbs grinding his barely remained tissues acted as a painful reminder and a mock of his past days as a slave. While Sidious was quite satisfied about his doings, Vader, however, was clearly aware that his rage towards the unfair treatments would only lead to his further fall to the abyss. Therefore, he chose to deliberately ignore the disgrace of repeated kneeling before others, and settle himself into a lamentable calmness , too enough to be numb. Now, He was too numb to realize the darkness that used to swallow everything no longer existed on the throne.

“You aren’t going to do that again, Father.”

Luke, whose limbs were slim but powerful, sounded tender and firm. He now radiated like a star.

“Please take your seat, Father.” He pointed the only seat in the throne room.

Vader lowered his helmet slightly. He said,”That’s your position, child.”

“No—None of us will need it,” his tone was as clear as water, “We will restore the Republic.”

Vader seated himself on the throne. Despite being sheltered by his life-maintaining suit, Vader could still feel the deepest coldness originated from what he was now sitting on. The darkness, which had been reigning here for decades, never faded away. He was exactly driven by the seduction of this darkness in his early years, which eventually turned himself into a killing machine. Gradually, however, he felt the coldness dissipating, for his star was closing in.

Luke could look into Vader’s eyes now, his height finally reached his father’s. He gazed at the dark helmet and the obscure lens, anyhow he did have a sense of being surrounded by a tenderness of clear blue. He tried to touch it from the Force Bond between himself and Vader, and then he gained a silent acceptance. So he seated himself on Vader’s thighs.

“Hurt?” Luke asked cautiously.

Vader didn’t answer, just opened his arms to embrace his child, softly.

It was a long missing scene between them, indeed.

Luke buried himself into his father’s chest. He could clearly distinguish the damaged but still powerful heartbeats from the chaotic breaths.

“The new Republic shall sentence my deserved fate.”

Neither sorrow nor happiness could be found in Vader’s tones. Actually he had already made his heart to accept his fateful endings and his trials. Also, his separation from his children, again.

“No……leave alone that. “

Both Luke’s body and his presence in the Force shivered a bit. He felt cold. He was by no means ignorant, he was just unwilling to accept it.

Out of solace, Vader hugged his child more tightly. Luke let out a slight sigh and closed his eyes.

Just take a rest, he thought.

Assuming it was a universe that let go of the control of time and destiny.

FIN.
